Holy Bridge

Founded in the early 1920, our church began as a small gathering of faithful believers. Over the years, it has grown into a strong spiritual community, rooted in worship, service, and fellowship

From humble beginnings to a thriving congregation, our journey reflects God’s faithfulness through every season. Through the years, the church has become a place of hope, healing, and connection—welcoming people from all walks of life. With a heart for outreach and spiritual growth, we continue to serve our community and spread the message of Christ with love and purpose.
